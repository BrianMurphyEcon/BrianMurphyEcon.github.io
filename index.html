About Me

Hi! My name is Brian Murphy. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in economics at the University of Houston. I am interested in labor, human capital, education, development, and health economics.

I have a B.A. and a B.S. in economics and mathematics from La Salle University in Philadelphia. I graduated Maxima Cum Laude, with a 4.0 GPA. During my undergrad, I was honored to receive the Flubacher Award, given to a student each year who shows commitment to community service and who excels in the classroom. Additionally, I was the recipient of the Geruson Award, given to the student who writes the most outstanding seminar paper. Finally, I was given the most outstanding student award in the College of Liberal Arts.
